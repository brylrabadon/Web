About Loan Tracker System

The Loan Tracker System is a simple and efficient tool designed to help you keep track of loans given to borrowers. You can easily add new loans, view their due dates, check their status (pending or overdue), and manage them with the delete option.

This system helps you stay organized and manage your loans effortlessly, ensuring you never lose track of important payment deadlines.

Features

  • Add loans with borrower name, amount, and due date.
  • Automatically marks loans as Overdue if past the due date.
  • View total number of loans and total loan amount.
  • Delete loans that are no longer needed.
  • Responsive and clean user interface.
